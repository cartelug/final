Last Updated: September 25, 2025

AccessUG ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website cartelug.com.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. All information provided is used exclusively by AccessUG to deliver the service you have requested.
