Step 2: Select Your TV Type

What type of TV setup do you have?

How to tell the difference:

  • Smart TV: Netflix app is directly on the TV. Has its own app store. Connects directly to internet (Wi-Fi/Cable).
  • Normal TV: Netflix app is on a device connected to your TV (e.g., Fire Stick, Mi Stick, Apple TV, PS5, Xbox, Decoder). You use that device's remote for Netflix.

Smart TV Setup

Easy Steps:

  1. 1️⃣ On your Smart TV, open the Netflix app and select Sign In.
  2. 2️⃣ Look for "Use Phone". This will display a code on your TV screen.
  3. 3️⃣ No code showing? If you only see fields for email and password, tap the Back button and choose the "Normal TV" setup instead.
  4. 4️⃣ See a code? Take a clear picture of the code (numbers or letters) on your TV screen.
  5. 5️⃣ Send the picture to us on WhatsApp. We'll help you complete the sign-in.
