How to tell the difference:

  • Smart TV: Netflix is an app you install from the TV’s own store (no external box needed).
  • Normal TV: Netflix runs on a connected device (Fire Stick, console, decoder, etc.).

Smart TV Setup

How to Start:

  1. Open Netflix on your TV and choose Sign In.
  2. Pick “Sign in with phone / web / another device”. A 6-digit or 8-letter CODE appears on-screen.
    If your TV only shows email / password fields, return and choose “Normal TV”.
  3. Snap a clear photo of that code.
  4. Send the photo to us on WhatsApp — we’ll activate your profile and text back your PIN.
