Access Netflix Temporary Code Emails

To get your Netflix temporary code, you'll need to view the emails we receive from Netflix. Select an option below for instructions on how to add the shared Gmail account to your device.

Important: You will need the password for this shared Gmail account. If you don't have it, please contact Cartelug support on WhatsApp to get the current password *before* proceeding.

Adding to Gmail in Web Browser

After Adding the Account:

Once the account is added to your Gmail app or browser, switch to its inbox. Look for recent emails from Netflix with a subject like "Your temporary access code" or similar. Open the latest relevant email and click the "Get Code" button inside it to see your Netflix temporary code.

Security Reminder:

For your security and ours, please remember to switch back to your personal Gmail account after use. Consider removing this shared account from your app/browser if you no longer need frequent access or when you've successfully used the code.
